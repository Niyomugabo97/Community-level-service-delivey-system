Platform Features

Everything you need to manage your community effectively

Umuganda Attendance

Track community work (Umuganda) attendance for all registered citizens. Mark present or absent, monitor participation rates, and generate attendance reports by location.

Inteko Attendance

Independently manage Inteko (community meetings) attendance with separate performance tracking, ensuring Umuganda and Inteko records never overlap.

School Management

Monitor school enrollment, track dropout rates, and analyze education statistics across sectors, cells, and villages to support data-driven interventions.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize attendance trends, sector-by-sector comparisons, village-level performance breakdowns, and school statistics through interactive charts and tables.

Infrastructure Reporting

Citizens and leaders can report damaged roads, broken facilities, and other infrastructure issues with location details and images for timely resolution.

Data Export

Download attendance records as CSV files with full details — name, sex, age, sector, cell, village, telephone, and date — for offline use and official reporting.

How It Works

Get started in three simple steps

1

Create an Account

Register as a leader, citizen, or school administrator. Your account is linked to your village, cell, and sector for location-based access.

2

Access Your Dashboard

Log in to your personalized dashboard. Leaders manage attendance and reports; citizens view their records; schools update enrollment data.

3

Track & Report

Record attendance session by session, file infrastructure reports when issues arise, and monitor community performance through live analytics.

4

Analyze Results

Visit the Analytics page to view charts, compare sectors, identify participation trends, and download data for official reporting.

5

Stay Informed

Check the News page for recent community activities, upcoming Umuganda and Inteko sessions, and the latest infrastructure repair updates.

6

Export & Share

Download attendance CSVs for any session, share analytics with sector leadership, and keep digital records for audits and reviews.
